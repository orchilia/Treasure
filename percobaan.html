TREASURE MAKER                                                                                           TREASURE MAKER

TREASURE (트레저) is a 10-member boy group under YG Entertainment. The group was formed through the reality show YG TREASURE BOX that aired from November 2018 - January 2019. They made their long-awaited debut on August 7, 2020 with the single album "The First Step : Chapter One". Eventually, it was announced that the group will become a permanent 12-member group and will be called TREASURE.
